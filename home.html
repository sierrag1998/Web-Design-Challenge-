Summary: Latitude v.s X

WeatherPy2 is an anaylsis of around 600 cities and their weather data on 01/05/17. After exploring several relationships between the weather data, the first conclusion drawn is the max temperatures of the cities tend to be higher in cities closer to the equator. More specifically, in the Northern Hemisphere, latititude and temperature have a negative correlation. In the Southern Hemisphere, it appears latititude and temperature have a postivie correlation. In addition, It appears in both hempispheres, there is not a significant correlation between the latitudes of cities and their humidities. Lastly, It appears in both hempispheres, there is not a significant correlation between the latitudes of cities and their windspeeds.
